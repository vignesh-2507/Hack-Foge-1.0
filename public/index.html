Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6) The United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 6 aims to ensure the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all by 2030. Key targets under this goal include: Universal Access: Ensuring everyone, everywhere has access to clean drinking water and safe sanitation facilities. Improved Water Quality: Reducing pollution and improving wastewater treatment and reuse. Water Efficiency: Promoting the efficient use of water in all sectors, especially in agriculture and industry.
